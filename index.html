About Lovely Cafe

Est. 2024 · Phnom Penh

Lovely Cafe was born from a simple belief — that a great cup of coffee can change your whole day. We source our beans from local farms, roast them fresh, and brew each cup with care and intention. We only use seasonal ingredients.

From our handcrafted espresso to our freshly baked pastries, everything on our menu is made with love and served with a smile. Come as you are — stay as long as you like.
